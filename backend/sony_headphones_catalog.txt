## Sony WH-1000XM5 - Premium Wireless Noise Cancelling Headphones

Price: Rs 29,990
Category: Over-Ear, Wireless, Noise Cancelling
Release Year: 2022

The Sony WH-1000XM5 is Sony's flagship wireless noise-cancelling headphone. It features two processors controlling eight microphones for unprecedented noise cancellation. The headphone uses a newly designed 30mm driver unit with a lightweight construction weighing only 250g.

Key Specifications:
- Driver: 30mm
- Frequency Response: 4 Hz - 40,000 Hz
- Battery Life: 30 hours with NC ON, 40 hours with NC OFF
- Charging: USB-C, 3 minutes charge = 3 hours playback
- Bluetooth: 5.2 with LDAC, AAC, SBC
- Active Noise Cancellation: Yes, Auto NC Optimizer
- Speak-to-Chat: Yes
- Multipoint Connection: Yes (2 devices simultaneously)
- Weight: 250g
- Colors: Black, Silver, Midnight Blue
- Foldable: No (flat-fold design)
- Headphone Type: Closed, Dynamic

The WH-1000XM5 uses Dual Noise Sensor Technology with two processors (Integrated Processor V1 + QN1) to cancel more noise than ever. The Auto NC Optimizer automatically detects your environment and optimizes noise cancelling. It supports Sony's LDAC codec for Hi-Res Audio wireless transmission up to 990kbps. The headphone also features Adaptive Sound Control that adjusts ambient sound settings based on your activity and location.

Best For: Premium noise cancellation, long flights, office use, audiophile-grade wireless listening
Pros: Best-in-class ANC, excellent sound quality, very comfortable, great call quality with 4 beamforming microphones
Cons: Does not fold flat for compact storage, expensive price point

## Comparison: Which Sony Headphone is Best for You?

For Noise Cancellation:
- Best Overall: WH-1000XM5 (over-ear) or WF-1000XM5 (true wireless)
- Best Value: WH-CH720N (over-ear) or WF-C700N (true wireless)

For Budget Under Rs 5,000:
- WH-CH520 at Rs 4,490 is the only option - great battery, no ANC

For Budget Under Rs 10,000:
- WH-CH720N (Rs 9,990) for over-ear with ANC
- WF-C700N (Rs 7,490) for true wireless with ANC
- MDR-7506 (Rs 8,499) for wired professional monitoring

For Gym and Workouts:
- WF-1000XM5 (premium, IPX4)
- WF-C700N (budget-friendly, IPX4)
- WF-1000XM4 (mid-range, IPX4)

For Phone Calls:
- WH-1000XM5 (4 beamforming mics, best call quality)
- WF-1000XM5 (bone conduction sensor for clear voice)

For Long Flights:
- WH-1000XM5 (30hr battery, best ANC)
- WH-1000XM4 (30hr battery, foldable for travel)

For Students / Work From Home:
- WH-CH720N (affordable ANC, lightweight, 35hr battery)
- WH-CH520 (cheapest, 50hr battery, no ANC)

For Audiophiles:
- WH-1000XM5 with LDAC (wireless Hi-Res)
- MDR-7506 (wired, flat studio response)
